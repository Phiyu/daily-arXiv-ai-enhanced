### [1] [Constraints on the inflationary vacuum and reheating era from NANOGrav](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05310)
*Debtosh Chowdhury,Rounak Nath,Sudipta Show*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: NANOGrav 15年数据暗示存在具有Hellings-Downs关联的随机引力波背景（SGWB），作者假设其源于暴胀，利用该信号约束暴胀参数（如张量谱指数nt、张量标量比r）及再热参数（状态方程ωre和温度Tre），发现偏好极蓝倾斜的张量谱和类辐射再热；进一步考虑非Bunch-Davies真空（特别是α真空），发现数据强烈限制α参数，并指出引入频率依赖的α可缓解蓝倾斜问题，未来引力波实验有望检验该机制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 近期NANOGrav等脉冲星计时阵列实验探测到具有Hellings-Downs角关联的公共红噪声信号，被解释为随机引力波背景（SGWB）的有力证据。该背景可能源于多种天体物理或宇宙学过程，其中暴胀起源是一个重要可能性。标准暴胀理论通常假设初始真空为Bunch-Davies真空，但其本质尚无定论。因此，本文旨在：1）利用最新NANOGrav数据约束暴胀及再热时期的物理参数；2）探索非Bunch-Davies初始真空（特别是α真空）对引力波谱的影响，并检验观测数据对此类新物理的限制能力。

Method: 作者基于NANOGrav 15年数据集提供的随机引力波背景（SGWB）信号证据，采用贝叶斯统计方法进行参数估计。首先，在标准暴胀框架下（假设Bunch-Davies真空），将观测到的引力波能谱与暴胀模型预测的张量扰动谱相联系，从而约束关键参数，包括张量谱指数（nt）、张量标量比（r）、再热状态方程（ωre）和再热温度（Tre）。其次，他们将分析扩展到非Bunch-Davies真空情形，具体引入了由参数α表征的α真空模型，计算其对原初引力波谱的修正，并再次利用NANOGrav数据拟合α参数。最后，他们探讨了一种更复杂的模型，即让α参数在某个阈值频率以上呈现频率依赖性，并评估该模型对解决标准模型中出现的极端蓝倾斜问题的有效性。

Result: 研究得出以下主要结果：1）在标准Bunch-Davies真空假设下，NANOGrav数据强烈偏好一个极端蓝倾斜的张量功率谱，张量谱指数为nt=2.20^{+0.36}_{-1.2}，并支持类辐射的再热过程（ωre=0.33^{+0.14}_{-0.36}）。2）当考虑非Bunch-Davies真空时，数据明确倾向于一种特定的α真空，并对真空参数α的取值范围给出了严格的限制。3）引入一个在高频段具有频率依赖性的α参数模型，能够有效缓解标准模型中张量谱过度蓝倾斜的问题，提供了一个潜在的解决方案。

Conclusion: NANOGrav观测到的随机引力波背景信号不仅为研究早期宇宙暴胀和再热物理提供了新窗口，还对暴胀初始条件这一基础问题提出了深刻见解。分析表明，标准Bunch-Davies真空下的暴胀模型面临张量谱过度蓝倾斜的挑战，而特定的非Bunch-Davies真空（α真空）不仅能更好地拟合当前数据，还能通过引入频率依赖性来解决这一理论难题。这凸显了脉冲星计时阵列作为探针，用于检验量子引力效应和早期宇宙初始条件的巨大潜力，未来更精确的引力波观测将能直接检验所提出的频率依赖α模型。

Abstract: NANOGrav and various pulsar timing array experiments recently reported evidence for a common red noise signal across millisecond pulsars. This signal exhibits Hellings-Downs inter-pulsar correlation patterns, providing compelling evidence for a stochastic gravitational wave background (SGWB) signal. In general, such a background can come from several astrophysical and cosmological phenomena. Assuming such SGWB has an inflationary origin, we use latest NANOGrav 15-year dataset to constrain the inflationary parameters e.g., tensor spectral index ($n_t$), tensor-to-scalar ratio ($r$), and explore the implications for the reheating phase through constraints on the reheating equation of state ($ω_{\text{re}}$) and reheating temperature ($T_{\text{re}})$. We find the preference for an extremely blue-tilted tensor spectrum $n_t=2.20^{+0.36}_{-1.2}$ and the radiation-like reheating scenario $ω_{\text{re}}=0.33^{+0.14}_{-0.36}$. Despite having no concrete evidence for the nature of the primordial vacua, the computation of gravitational wave (GW) sourced by tensor perturbations assumes the inflationary vacuum to be a Bunch-Davies vacuum. In this work, we examine modifications to the GW spectrum originating from the non-Bunch-Davies primordial vacuum. We find that NANOGrav observations favour a specific type of non-Bunch-Davies vacuum, known as the alpha-vacuum. Furthermore, our analysis demonstrates that the observations strikingly narrow down the range of the parameter $α$ characterizing the vacua. On top of that, we find that a frequency-dependent parametrization of the vacuum parameter $α$ beyond a threshold frequency can yield a minimal solution to alleviate the blue-titled issue. Finally, we highlight the possibility of testing such frequency dependence of $α$ by probing the GW spectrum through future GW experiments.

### [2] [The multiple corrugations in the Galactic disk derived from the LAMOST and Gaia survey data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05937)
*Jifei Wang,Zhuohan Li,Chengdong Li,Yuqin Chen,Chengqun Yang,Zixi Guo,Zhou Fan,Hongrui Gu,Maoli Bu*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究通过分析LAMOST和Gaia的大样本恒星数据，发现银河系薄盘内外区域存在明显的运动学与化学丰度过渡，并提出径向褶皱模型可合理解释观测到的Rg-Vphi-VR空间中的波动特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解银河系盘结构中观测到的波状特征的物理起源，特别是探索内外薄盘之间的过渡机制以及径向褶皱是否能解释这些现象。

Method: 结合LAMOST DR8与Gaia DR3的数据，利用空间位置、运动学和化学丰度信息，构建一个由两个反向传播径向波组成的简化褶皱模型，并通过N体模拟验证模型的有效性。

Result: 在观测数据中确认了Rg-Vphi-VR平面上的波状模式；识别出内外盘在旋转速度和金属丰度上的明显过渡；模型成功复现了径向速度VR随银心距的周期性变化，且模拟结果与观测一致。

Conclusion: 径向褶皱为银河系盘中观测到的动力学特征提供了合理的物理解释，表明银河系盘可能受到多个扰动源影响，未来需借助新一代巡天进一步研究。

Abstract: Large spectroscopic and astrometric surveys have revealed complex wave-like features in the Milky Way disk, suggesting that its kinematic and chemical structures are shaped by time-dependent perturbations. Recent studies have reported oscillatory patterns in the Rg-Vphi-VR space, hinting at a possible structural transition in the outer disk. We aim to characterise the transition between the inner and outer Galactic thin disk and to investigate whether radial corrugations can provide a plausible physical interpretation of the observed features. We analysed two large stellar samples from LAMOST DR8 and Gaia DR3, combining spatial, kinematic, and chemical diagnostics. A simplified corrugation model consisting of two radial waves propagating in opposite directions was constructed and fitted to the observed VR pattern. We further validated the model using N-body simulations. Both LAMOST and Gaia samples reproduce the previously reported wave-like pattern in the Rg-Vphi-VR plane. We identify a clear transition between the inner and outer disks via the variations in rotational velocity and metallicities. The corrugation model naturally reproduces the periodic variation of VR with galactocentric radius, and the superposition of the inward and outward propagating modes gives rise to a comparable oscillatory pattern in both observations and simulations. Our modelling suggests that radial corrugations can provide a plausible interpretation of the observed kinematic signatures. The results highlight the complex, multi-perturber nature of the Galactic disk and motivate further investigation with upcoming surveys.

### [3] [A statistical look on kinematic planes of satellite galaxies II: The physics behind their early formation in TNG50 MW/M31-like galaxies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05972)
*Matías Gámez-Marín,Rosa Domínguez-Tenreiro,Isabel Santos-Santos,Diego Sotillo-Ramos,Alexander Knebe*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究利用TNG50模拟，分析了190个类银河系/仙女座星系系统中运动学持久平面（KPPs）的物理起源，发现约67%的KPP卫星轨道极与拉格朗日体积最强坍缩方向对齐，并表明KPP是高红移宇宙网各向异性质量坍缩的遗迹。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解卫星星系在运动学上呈现持久共面结构（KPPs）的物理机制，尤其是在ΛCDM宇宙学框架下，这些结构是否源于早期宇宙网的各向异性坍缩过程。

Method: 基于TNG50模拟中的190个MW/M31类宿主-卫星系统，追踪其周围拉格朗日体积（LV）的演化；采用简化惯量张量分析LV主坍缩方向的时间演化，并将其与卫星轨道极的聚集进行关联；同时进行KPP内卫星运动的运动学分析。

Result: 约67%的KPP卫星轨道极与LV最强坍缩方向（e₃）对齐，约20%与中间轴（e₂）对齐，与随机分布显著不同；卫星垂直和径向运动早期衰减，形成旋转主导的盘状构型；卫星共面、轨道极聚集与LV形状演化的时间尺度基本同步，峰值出现在宇宙年龄约4 Gyr时。

Conclusion: 早期KPP是高红移时期由局域宇宙网驱动的各向异性质量坍缩过程所留下的“化石”遗迹，支持ΛCDM模型下大尺度结构对卫星星系空间分布的决定性影响。

Abstract: We investigate the physical origin of kinematically persistent planes (KPPs) of satellite galaxies in a sample of 190 Milky Way (MW)/M31-like host-satellite systems drawn from the TNG50 simulation. Building on the identification of 46 early KPPs in a previous work, we analyse their formation in the context of the high-redshift evolution of the local Cosmic Web by tracking the deformation of the so-called Lagrangian Volumes (LVs) surrounding each system. Using a reduced tensor-of-inertia analysis, we characterise the time evolution of the principal directions of collapse and relate them to the clustering of satellite orbital poles. We find that in approximately 67\% of KPPs satellite orbital poles align with the LV direction of strongest collapse, $\vec{e}_3$, while a smaller fraction ($\sim20\%$) align with the intermediate axis, $\vec{e}_2$; alignments with the major axis are rare. These alignments are statistically distinct from random expectations and reflect the confinement of satellites to planar configurations normal to the corresponding LV principal directions. We perform a kinematic analysis of satellite motion within KPPs, finding that vertical and radial motions relative to these KPPs decay early, leading to rotation-dominated, ``disky'' configurations. The characteristic timescales for satellites to settle onto a common orbital plane, for satellite orbital pole clustering, and for LV shape evolution are found to be quasi-coeval, peaking at a Universe age T$_{\rm uni}\sim4$~Gyr, during the fast mass assembly phase of the host halo. These results support a scenario in which early KPPs are fossil remnants of high-redshift, anisotropic mass collapse driven by the local Cosmic Web formation process in $Λ$CDM.

</details>
