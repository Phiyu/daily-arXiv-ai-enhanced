Conclusion: 观测显示暗物质晕核心的 Γ ≈ 1.7（d=3 时处于禁戒区间中部），因此超轻或类流体暗物质模型难以自然解释此类结构，除非存在显著的其他天体物理效应。

As a consequence for cosmology, it is worth noting that observations favor a core in the centers of dark matter halos with $Γ\simeq 1.7$, which (for $d=3$) lies approximately in the middle of the excluded range. Therefore, proposals such as ultra-light or fluid-like dark matter models are essentially ruled out as natural explanations for halo cores, provided other astrophysical effects are negligible.
