TL;DR: 研究通过改进银河系化学演化模型，利用GAM方法估计恒星诞生半径，并结合Gaia和Galpy数据揭示了银河系恒星迁移的主要机制：约75%的恒星受角动量变化主导的'churning'影响，其余主要受'eccentric orbits'相关的'blurring'或未受显著扰动，不同化学丰度群体现不同迁移模式，可能与银河系棒和旋臂结构相互作用有关。

Motivation: 明确银河系恒星轨道演化中churning与blurring两种机制的相对主导作用，特别是利用化学演化模型和动力学数据结合的方式探究恒星迁移的历史。

Method: 构建更精细、覆盖更广年龄范围的银河系化学演化模型网格，使用广义加性模型（GAM）估算超出传统金属丰度分箱模型范围的恒星诞生半径，并结合Gaia的视差与自行数据及Galpy计算的动力学参数进行分析；通过21种化学丰度的层次聚类对恒星样本按金属丰度分组。

Result: 发现富金属恒星多形成于内盘并外迁，贫金属恒星则形成于外盘并内迁；约75%的恒星表现出churning特征，其余主要为未受扰动或由blurring主导；不同化学丰度群的迁移模式存在差异。

Conclusion: churning是银河系恒星轨道演化的主要机制，但不同化学群之间的差异反映了其与银河系结构（如棒和旋臂）相互作用的复杂历史。

Abstract: As stars traverse the Milky Way, their orbits evolve through perturbations that alter their orbital radii. These changes arise from two mechanisms: churning, which modifies angular momentum, and blurring, which induces eccentric orbits without major angular momentum change. To assess whether churning or blurring dominates the dynamical evolution of Gaia-ESO stars, we refine Galactic chemical-evolution models by constructing finer grids that span a wider age range. Using a generalised additive model (GAM), we estimate stellar birth radii beyond the limits of binned metallicity models and compare them with dynamical parameters derived from Gaia parallaxes and proper motions, and Galpy. Our metallicity-stratified sample, grouped through hierarchical clustering of 21 chemical abundances, reveals clear migratory signatures: metal-rich stars formed in the inner disc preferentially move outwards, while more metal-poor stars formed at larger radii tend to migrate inwards. About 75% of stars show signs of churning, while the remainder are largely undisturbed or shaped by blurring. These patterns vary among chemical groups, likely reflecting interactions with the Galactic bar and spiral arms.
