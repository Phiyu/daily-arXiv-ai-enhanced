Method: 采用随机Hopf振子模型耦合气体密度G与恒星形成率S；用Euler–Maruyama方法引入暗物质晕属性散射作为乘性噪声；通过径向约化导出Fokker–Planck方程；结合差动剪切Ω(r)和空间变化的分岔参数模拟形态与AGN反馈。

Driving the growth parameter sub-critical ($r_{agn} < 0$) yields ``Red and Dead'' cores via attractor collapse. Dark matter halo scatter suppresses mean star formation while enhancing intermittency, offering a minimal yet interpretable framework linking local feedback and global potentials to macroscopic galactic evolution.

Method: 基于作用量变量J构造解析分布函数f(J)，分别处理各向同性、径向偏置球形系统及具显著速度各向异性的扁平系统；结合自洽建模与线性稳定性分析研究动力学不稳定性。

Result: 明确了热成分DFs在J_φ→0时应满足的物理条件；给出了多种几何与各向异性情形下f(J)的构造方法；发现自洽的径向偏置球形系统普遍存在四极扰动不稳定性；混沌机制可能在绝热盘增长过程中维持该约束。

We explain why such models have unphysical velocity distributions unless the DFs of hot components satisfy certain conditions as J_φ-> 0. We show how DFs for both isotropic and radially biased spherical systems can be constructed with specified f(J). Construction of self-consistent models rather than populations that are confined by an external potential leads to the conclusion that radially-biased spherical systems are generically unstable to quadrupolar perturbations. Chaos is likely key to maintenance of these constraints during adiabatic disc growth.

Moreover, in the positive curvature scenario, we obtain that the assumption of a constant Ricci scalar is not consistent, but we are able to find analytical solutions in approximated regimes. In all cases we have found that the general behavior of the probabilities already found for Einstein Gravity is preserved, including the prediction of a non-singular initial state due to quantum corrections, even though the probabilities increase or decrease in a model dependent way.
